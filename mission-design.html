Challenge Tasks
Task 1 — Get to orbit Essential
Set your destination to Moon. Adjust propellant and dry mass until your delta-v available exceeds the required amount. What is the minimum propellant fraction needed?
Task 2 — Mars mission trade-off Advanced
Switch to Mars. The mission needs ~14,200 m/s. Try: (a) increasing Isp to 450 to simulate a hydrogen engine — how much propellant does this save? (b) Halve the payload — how does the delta-v margin change? Record both results.
Task 3 — Outer planets Expert
Try Europa or Titan. These missions require enormous delta-v. Using the rocket equation above, calculate what mass ratio would be needed mathematically. Is a single-stage mission feasible? Why do real missions use gravity assists?
